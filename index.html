About Me

Welcome to my portfolio. Here you’ll find bits of what I do as a structural engineer and what interests me within the field.

At the heart of who I am is truly a learner. I like to thoroughly understand everything I do and seek ways to improve my designs with that understanding. What often follows are design tools I create to make life easier and more efficient for me and my colleagues. As a practicing structural engineer, I appreciate the frustrations with the software we use every day. Because of that, I make my tools to be flexible enough to handle the unique scenarios we encounter.
